Tips

Origin:

creating invisible animation loops (…a few tips):

  1. the longer the animation the better
    longer animations allow you to create a more complex performance. They also give you an opportunity to add a lot of subtle nuances to your motion.

  2. avoid big, easy-to-spot motions
    if he was running and then suddenly waved his arm, you would easily remember seeing that arm wave. You want to stick to subtle motions that aren’t visually easy to read/remember.

  3. animations in motion are better
    motion camouflages the loop really well vs an animation that comes to a complete stop.

  4. use cameras
    camera animation is super useful to tell a story/stage your shot but you can also use it in a way that allows you to misdirect the viewers eye so that they can’t spot the loop.
